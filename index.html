What i do.

In a world full of devices and interfaces, attention is scarce and precious. That’s why we need to be extremely carefull on how to use this brief moment of focus. By studying the complete customer journey and converting this into meaningful wireframes/prototypes/flowcharts, making sure the attention of your users will not be wasted by bad design choices.
Converting the designed wireframes, prototypes and flowcharts into effective and user-friendly webpages through HTML / CSS / JS and WordPress CMS. Currently learning more advanced front-end frameworks like Vue.JS and React.
Creating all kinds of content suitable for your marketing & branding campaign, like videoproduction, editing, animations and more.
Creating a strategy to reach your target audience in a way that makes them remember it, by designing with the 'wow' factor in mind. If you want to make a lasting impression on your users, make sure you invest into creative concepts.

Project: Eichholtz

Webshop / WooCommerce / WordPress

Luxury furniture brand Eichholtz is expanding to The Netherlands, and asked us to build a webshop containing more than 3000 products. We've built a custom WooCommerce shop that reflects their branding and tone of voice.

Project: Premium Mondkapjes

Branding / WooCommerce / WordPress

When a pandemic like COVID-19 strikes and facemasks are made mandatory all over the world, it's important to act fast. Premium-mondkapjes.nl came to us needing a webshop as quick as possible. We made it happen, so everyone can buy high-quality and comfortable masks through this webshop.

Project: Spaze

WordPress / Appstore & Playstore

Spaze is an intelligent way to manage crowds and queues. This idea originated when stores needed to close due to lockdown measurements, and shopowners wanted to give customers insight in their queues and waittimes. Spaze released an app on both iOS and Android, and we designed the accompanying website for them.
